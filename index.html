FAQs

Who are we?
MarketMirror is a platform that provides farmers with real and reliable market prices for their crops. By reducing dependence on middlemen and guesswork, we help farmers make informed decisions and receive fair value for their produce. Our goal is to make pricing transparent, simple, and accessible to every farmer
MarketMirror gathers live price data from mandis and verified market reports, filters it for accuracy, and presents it in a clear and easy format. The platform also uses AI to study price trends and offer helpful insights, so farmers can decide the best time and place to sell..
